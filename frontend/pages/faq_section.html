Do I have to create a workspace before creating/uploading documents?

No. When you create an account, a default workspace is created with the name of "Personal Workspace". So you must have at least one workspace in order to start uploading your own documents.

I deleted my account by mistake... Can I get back all my documents stored in my workspaces?

No. Unfortunately, once your account has been deleted, all the data stored and linked to this account are removed from our servers.
