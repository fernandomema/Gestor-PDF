How do you edit a PDF?

As we've already stated, there's no need to install or download any software. To get started, drag and drop the PDF you want to edit and follow the instruccions below:

  1. Once you have selected the PDF you would like to edit, you will see a variety of editing tools that InstaPDF free editor offers.
  2. If you need to add text to the PDF, the "Text" function allows you to add new text to your PDFs.
  3. If you need to erase some text, the "Eraser" function will do it for you.
  4. If you need to sign your PDF, just click the "Sign" tool. Once clicked, you will see a couple of few options: signing the PDF by typing your name on the keyboard or drawing your signature with your desktop mouse.
